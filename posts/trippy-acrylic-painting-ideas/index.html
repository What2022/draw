How Do I Shade A Drawing In Sketchbook?#

There are a few ways to shade a drawing in a sketchbook. One way is to use a lightening bolt method. You would place a lightening bolt on the drawing and then shade it with your hand. Another way is to use a light pencil and use the back of your hand to shade the drawing. If you’re working on a drawing in a sketchbook, it’s important to shade it correctly so that the drawing looks its best. Here’s how:

  1. Shade the drawing using a light hand. Use a light hand to shade the drawing by painting each color over the top of the other. This will create a range of tones in your drawing and make it look more realistic.

  2. Use dark layers to shade the darker colors. Shade each color in turn, making sure that each layer has an equal amount of darkness and light. This will give your drawing a natural look and feel.
