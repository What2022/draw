Some digital artists prefer to use a shading method that is known as “doubling” or “triple-shot.“Doubling works by duplicating the colors of one image into two or three different images, then blending these together to create a more realistic shading effect. Triple-shot shading is similar, but rather than duplicating the colors, it creates three separate images with different color tones and then blends them together to create an overall shade.
