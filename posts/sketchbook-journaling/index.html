How Do You Draw A Realistic Eye?#

There are a few ways to draw a realistic eye. One way is to use a light pencil to sketch out the outline of the eye using a light line. Then, use a dark pencil to fill in the details of the eye, such as the iris and pupil. Finally, use a hard brush to give the eye its final look. There is no one definitive way to draw a realistic eye, but some tips on how to do it can help improve your artistry. First and foremost, always keep in mind the principles of perspective when drawing eyes - make them look large and round, rather than small and pointed. Additionally, try to use light and shadows well when depicting eyes - they should be dark or light depending on the angle you’re looking from. Finally, take into account the position of the iris when drawing - it should be near the pupil, or near the outside of the eye if you have a closed eye.
