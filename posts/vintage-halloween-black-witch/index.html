The three basic shading techniques are: light, dark, and indirect. Traditional shading techniques are a way of adding color to an image by using light and dark colors. -Blending Modes: Another traditional shading technique is blending modes. Blending modes allow you to change the opacity of pixels in an image, which can affect the appearance of the result. These modes include luminosity, hue, saturation, and brightness.
