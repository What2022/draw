Why Do Art Pencils Not Have Erasers?#

Art pencils do not have erasers. This is because erasers are used to remove markings from paper, and art pencils are not meant to be used for that purpose. There are a few reasons why art pencils do not have erasers. The main reason is that they are dependant on the artist’s hand to make sure the lead is evenly used. If an artist’s hand is too shaky, the lead may not be evenly used and create lines or artefacts on the paper. Additionally, when an artist wants to erase something, it is usually easier done with a pen than a pencil because a pen has a sharper point and can easily remove any markings or errors that may have been made while drawing with a pencil.
