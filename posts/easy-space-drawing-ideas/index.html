  1. Use a light pencil to outline the inside of your mouth in a thin line. This will give your mouth a more natural look.
  2. Use curves and contours to make your mouth more realistic. For example, add a jagged line down the center of your lower lip or create circles around your eyes.
  3. Add details such as furrows in your brow, wrinkles on your forehead, or even moustache growths if you want to make your character more different-looking than the others in the story.
