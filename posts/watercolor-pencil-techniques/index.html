Light and shadow: Giving your drawing depth Light and shadow are important aspects of drawing. They can give your drawing depth and make it more interesting. To create light and shadow in your drawing, you will need to use a light source. This can be either natural or artificial light. Once you have your light source, you can start to create shadows.

Shadows are created when an object blocks the light from reaching another object. The darker the shadow, the greater the contrast between the two objects. To make your shadows more realistic, you can use a variety of techniques such as cross hatching or stippling.
