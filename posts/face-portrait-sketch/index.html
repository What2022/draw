What Are Shading Techniques?#

Shading techniques are the methods used to create a realistic environment for your artwork. They can be used to create a variety of effects, including lighting, shadows, and highlights. Shading techniques are a way of adding or subtracting color from an image to create a desired effect. They can be used in paintings, photos, illustrations, and web graphics. There are many different shading techniques, and each has its own advantages and disadvantages. Here are three of the most common shading techniques: Linear Gradient: This shading technique is used to add depth to an image by dividing the image into bands of equal thickness. The linear gradient starts with the darkest tone and Adds lightness in ascending steps, until the final tone is lightest.
